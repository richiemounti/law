About Us

Guya Advocates is a boutique modern law firm providing specialized services within the commercial and business spheres. We provide our clients with critical legal expertise with commercial nous and strategic thinking. We are consultants, specialists, and solution providers. Our aim is to provide cost effective legal advice while maintaining the quality of our output. Our team comprises of legal experts in the key disciplines of business law all under one roof. We are best placed to answer to your concerns and provide sound legal solutions ranging from investigation, consultancy and dispute resolution.
